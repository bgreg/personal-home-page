About

Senior Software Engineer with a track record of taking products from concept to reality. I specialize in clean, maintainable code and systems that solve real-world problems at scale. I'm known for making sense of complexity by digging deeper, researching the contexts, surfacing hidden edge cases, and turning code into reliable tools and easy-to-maintain resources teams can build on. As a team lead, I've helped cross-functional teams deliver high-impact features, mentored junior developers, and championed best practices that stick. Outside of work, I run Ruby meetups, lead technical book clubs, and promote a culture of learning and inclusivity in tech. I balance my engineering work with hands-on crafts like shoemaking and bartending, where small details matter and mastery takes time. These creative disciplines remind me that whether I'm debugging code, shaping leather, or mixing flavors, the goal is the same: to create something that is a pleasure to experience. I spend a lot of time thinking about where technology is headed: AI and the new shape of human-computer collaboration, quantum computing and what it will mean for the systems we build today. I read widely and always try to bring new perspectives to hard problems.
